## RECORDING NOTES:

### Pace and Timing:

- Speak at a moderate and clear speed
- Natural pauses between sections
- Give viewers time to understand each feature

### Mouse Movements:

- Move slowly and deliberately
- Highlight important items with smooth clicks and scrolls
- Keep the cursor visible to guide attention

### Areas to Focus:

- Zoom or highlight main buttons (Read, Add to vocabulary)
- Show smooth animations when changing sections
- Use bright colors to highlight interactive elements

### Technical Recommendations:

- Minimum resolution: 1920x1080
- Recording speed: 60 FPS if possible
- Good quality microphone
- Clean and professional background
- Add light background music optional
